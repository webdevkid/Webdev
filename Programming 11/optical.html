Optical Illusions

Understanding Optical Illusions

Optical illusions manipulate visual perception through clever use of shapes, colors, and patterns. They exploit the brain's assumptions about the world, creating impossible figures or ambiguous images. Famous examples include the Rubin Vase and the Penrose Triangle.
